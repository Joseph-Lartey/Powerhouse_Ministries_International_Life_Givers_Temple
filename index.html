ABOUT US

Life Givers Temple is a place of worship and spiritual growth, dedicated to fostering a community of faith, love, and service. Our mission is to empower individuals to live fulfilling lives through the teachings of Jesus Christ.

Through prayer, outreach, and the power of the Word, we inspire people to build a deeper relationship with God and positively impact their communities.
